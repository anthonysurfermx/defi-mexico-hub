“I Should” is the problem. Change it to “I Must”

2 602
Most traders don’t have a strategy problem. They have a commitment problem.
They live in “should”. I should follow my plan. I should wait. I should review.

But “should” is weak. It leaves space for hesitation. And the market punishes hesitation every time.

The shift happens when “should” becomes “must”. That’s where things start to change.

📋 1.Trading Plan Is Non-Negotiable
A trading plan is not something you should have. It’s something you must follow.
Without it, you’re reacting. Clicking buttons, chasing moves, trading for the
Every trade should be decided before it happens. If it’s not in your plan, it doesn’t exist. ✅ 2. Checklist Filters Your Trades
Most traders have a checklist, but they don’t respect it. That’s why they take average setups and convince themselves it’s good enough. A checklist protects your capital from you. Keep it simple. Clear conditions. If one thing is missing, you stay out. 🔎 3.Review Every Trade
Not just the wins. Every trade. This is where real growth happens. Your losses are not the problem. Ignoring them is. Sit down in silence and go through your trades like a professional. Journal everything.That’s where you see the truth. 📈 4.Backtesting Builds Confidence
Backtesting is daily work. It builds pattern recognition. You start seeing the same behavior again and again. That’s where real confidence comes from. Not from guessing, but from repetition and data. 💪 5.Strong Decision to Succeed
Traders are trying to to make money online. But really never committed to do whatever it takes to succeed. Don't let others unsuccessful story discourage you. It's your life. The fact they failed is not your problem. You will make it. 💯 6. Isolation Creates Progress
Most traders never give themselves space to grow. Too much noise. Too many distractions. Spend time in full focus. No scrolling. No outside input. Just you and your process. That’s where progress speeds up, try my 90 day Monk Mode Protocol 👇
‼️Yes, it's only 6 habits. Tell me in the comments which another 3 would you add.

And then ask yourself honestly.

Where are you still operating in “should”?

Because that’s exactly where you are holding yourself back.

Turn it into “must”.

That’s where things start to change.
