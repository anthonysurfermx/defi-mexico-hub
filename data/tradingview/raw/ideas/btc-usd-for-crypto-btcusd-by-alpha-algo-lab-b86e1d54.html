BTC\USD

PRICe delivered a clean sell-side liquidity sweep below the external range low (ERL), taking out weak hands and triggering stops. This move signals a shift in order flow as the market transitions from discount back toward premium.

Following the sweep, price began forming higher lows and showing internal strength, suggesting accumulation after manipulation. With sell-side liquidity now taken, the logical next objective is buy-side liquidity, sitting in the premium range (IRL).

The current narrative is straightforward:
Liquidity taken → displacement → continuation toward IRL

🧠 Key Concepts:

ERL (External Range Liquidity) = previous swing high/low

IRL (Internal Range Liquidity) = inefficiencies / FVG targets

Bias flips bullish after sell-side sweep

🎯 Trade Idea:

Bias: Bullish

Entry: Discount zone after sweep (~69.3k–69.8k)

Stop Loss: Below ERL low (~68.8k)

Target: IRL / premium (~74.5k–75.5k)

⚡ Summary:

Market cleared liquidity on the downside and is now positioned to rebalance inefficiencies higher. As long as price holds above the swept low, continuation toward premium remains the most probable path.
