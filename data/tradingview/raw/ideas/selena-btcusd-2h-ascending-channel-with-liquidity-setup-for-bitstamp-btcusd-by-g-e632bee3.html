Selena | BTCUSD · 2H – Ascending Channel with Liquidity Setup

225
3 days ago
BTCUSD

After a strong bullish expansion from the 63K region, price entered a structured uptrend within the ascending channel. The recent move toward the 74K area indicates a potential liquidity grab above previous highs. However, this zone also aligns with resistance and prior rejection levels, making it a critical decision point. If buyers sustain momentum, a breakout could lead to continuation toward higher targets. Otherwise, rejection from this zone may trigger a corrective move toward the lower boundary of the channel.
