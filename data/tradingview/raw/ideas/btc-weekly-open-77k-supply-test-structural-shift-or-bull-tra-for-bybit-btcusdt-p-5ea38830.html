BTC Weekly Open: $77K Supply Test — Structural Shift or Bull Tra

105
4 days ago
BTC opened the week with displacement — pushing to $74K after weeks of slow grinding. The structure has shifted and we're now approaching the level that will define the rest of March.

Structure recap:

The 4H has flipped bullish. Starting from the $66K CHoCH, price has printed a series of BOS confirmations — each one validating the structural shift. This isn't a slow grind anymore, it's displacement with intent.

The decision zone — $77–77.4K:

There's a major HTF supply OB sitting at $77–$77.4K from the February distribution. This is the zone where institutions were selling before the breakdown. It hasn't been tested since — first touch of unmitigated supply is where we find out if this move is real.

Break through $77.4K with displacement = structural shift confirmed. Path opens to $83K+ where the next supply sits.

Rejection at $77K with LTF CHoCH = the move up was a liquidity grab. Price likely rotates back into the range and the $69K equal lows below become the target.

What's supporting the bulls:

The CHoCH at $66K is the structural anchor — bullish as long as that holds. Multiple BOS on the 4H show sustained buying, not a single spike. Demand OB at $72.8–$73K is the nearest support if we get a pullback before the $77K test.

What's supporting the bears:

HTF trend is still technically bearish until $77.4K gets reclaimed. Last week's staircase grind built a wall of breakout longs with stops below — that liquidity hasn't been taken. A rejection from $77K supply creates the perfect setup for a sweep of the $69K lows.

What matters:

How price reacts at $77K tells us everything. Displacement through = bullish. Rejection with structure break = bearish. Everything between here and there is just positioning.

Structure first. Always.

Update — March 18, Post-FOMC

The bear scenario is playing out. Price pushed above $76K but couldn't reach the $77–$77.4K HTF supply OB — rejected before even testing it. That's weakness, not strength.

Today: 1H ChoCH at $73.8K, BOS lower, sitting at $71.2K post-Powell. The demand OB at $72.8–$73K I flagged as nearest support? Broken clean. Now resistance on any bounce.

Powell added macro pressure — inflation elevated, no cuts without progress, Middle East energy risk, "difficult situation." Nothing here supports a reversal.

The $69K equal lows I mentioned as the bear target are now in play. If that liquidity gets swept and we see LTF structure shift back bullish, that's the long entry. Until then, bearish.
