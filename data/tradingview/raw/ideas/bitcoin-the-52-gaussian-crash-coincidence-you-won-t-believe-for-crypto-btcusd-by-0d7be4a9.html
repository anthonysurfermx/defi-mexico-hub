BITCOIN The -52% Gaussian crash 'coincidence' you won't believe!

4 248
No fancy multi indicator analysis here. No elaborate terminology. Just one indicator, which makes Bitcoin (BTCUSD) crash every time it flashes.

That's the Gaussian Channel (GC), which we haven't used in a long time but every time it historically turned red since the 2014 Bear Cycle, BTC crashed by a little over -52.00% from that moment.

And it just turned red this week. In 2014 once it did, the market crashed by another -52.87%. In 2018, it crashed by -52.55% and in 2022 by -52.31%. So if we count -52.00% from this week's High, we get $36500 and that's not an unreasonable bottom projection based on that very reliable model.
