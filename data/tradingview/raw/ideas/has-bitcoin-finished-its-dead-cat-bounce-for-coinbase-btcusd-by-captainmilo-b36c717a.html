Has Bitcoin finished its dead cat bounce?

138
After reaching 76K, Bitcoin faced a strong rejection and has now fallen below the 71K support level.

This could be an early sign of a potential downtrend, possibly targeting 55K or even lower.

The next key support sits around 66K, which also aligns with the last major trendline support.

Bulls need to reclaim 71K to regain momentum, otherwise the next critical opportunity for a bounce may come near 66K.”
