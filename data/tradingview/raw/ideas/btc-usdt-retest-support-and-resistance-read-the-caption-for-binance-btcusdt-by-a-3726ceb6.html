BTC/USDT | Retest, support and resistance (READ THE CAPTION)

34
As you can see, ever since failing to make it out of the Wick, Bitcoin fist started to consolidate and then heavily dropped in price, from 76,000 to 68,800, reaching the 4H FVG Low, but it bounced back and closed inside the 4H FVG. Afterwards, it started to recover a bit, currently being traded at around 70,600.

BTCUSDT retested the 2H FVG, but it dropped back down again, currently it's stuck between the 2 FVGs. So far, the High of the 4H FVG has been acting as an support for Bitcoin, but it has to go through the 2H FVG next which is a strong resistance zone right now.

I expect BTC to retest the 2H FVG, and if it goes above the FVG's C.E. at 71,720 and stabilizes there, we could see it to go to retest the Wick, at 72,945.

But if it drops and reaches the 4H FVG and the support fails, we could see it reaching 68,800 and a bit lower to sweep the Liquidity below the 68,793 level.
