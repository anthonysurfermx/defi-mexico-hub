BTC 4H – Channel Rejection & Return to Mid-Structure

58
Bitcoin is trading inside a well-defined ascending channel on the 4H timeframe.

After pushing into the upper boundary near the 75K region, price printed a strong rejection and rotated lower, breaking short-term internal structure on the way down.

Now BTC is approaching mid-channel and rising support around the 68.8K–69.5K region.

Structure Context:
• Ascending channel still intact
• Sharp rejection from upper boundary
• Breakdown of short-term higher-low sequence
• Price testing internal support

As long as BTC holds above the rising channel support, the broader structure remains constructive.

A bounce from this region would suggest another rotation inside the channel.

However, a clean breakdown below rising support would invalidate the channel and open room for a deeper correction toward lower liquidity pockets.

For now, BTC is reacting at structural support. This is where continuation vs. breakdown gets decided.
