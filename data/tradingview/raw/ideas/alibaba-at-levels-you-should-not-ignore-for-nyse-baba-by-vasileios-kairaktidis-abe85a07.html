📊 Alibaba (BABA) – Technical Setup & Investment View

Alibaba Group

On a purely technical basis, we believe Alibaba presents a compelling buying opportunity at current levels.

🧠 Background

The stock has declined significantly from its peak, primarily due to:

Regulatory pressure in China (2021 crackdown)

Slowing growth expectations

Broader sentiment shift away from Chinese equities

In 2021, Alibaba entered a major correction, breaking key support levels and falling back to price ranges last seen around 2015 (sub-$60 levels).

📉 Structure Since Then

Since bottoming, the stock:

Entered a prolonged consolidation phase

Built a strong base over multiple years

Eventually broke out of this consolidation in 2025

Following the breakout, Alibaba traded within a clearly defined ascending channel (marked in purple), reflecting improving sentiment and trend strength.

⚠️ Recent Price Action (March 2026)

In March 2026, the stock broke below the lower boundary of the ascending channel.



However, we view this not as structural weakness, but as:

👉 A natural retest of the prior consolidation resistance, which has now turned into a key support level.

📊 Why the Stock Fell Recently

The recent pullback appears to be driven by a combination of:

Short-term profit-taking after the strong 2025 rally

Broader volatility in Chinese equities

Macro uncertainty (rates, global growth expectations)

Profit Collapse: Adjusted net income fell to RMB 15.63–16.7 billion

High Cost of Strategic Pivot

👉 Importantly, this move looks technical rather than fundamental deterioration

📈 Current Setup

Alibaba is now revisiting:

👉 A major support zone formed by prior consolidation resistance

At the same time:

Momentum remains constructive

Sentiment is stabilizing

Analysts continue to project upside

🎯 Valuation Context

Current analyst targets:

High: ~$237

Low: ~$121–140

Median: ~$195

👉 This implies:

Limited downside from current levels

Attractive risk/reward profile

🔥 Investment Thesis

We believe:

The current level represents a high-probability support zone

The recent breakdown is likely a false move / retest

The stock is positioned for a potential reversal

⚠️ Strategy

We are monitoring for:

Stabilization at current levels

Confirmation of support holding

Early signs of trend reversal

👉 Upon confirmation, we view Alibaba as a strong buy candidate

🏁 Final Take

👉 Alibaba is at a critical technical level where risk/reward is skewed to the upside

We are not buying the breakout, we are buying the retest of strength.
