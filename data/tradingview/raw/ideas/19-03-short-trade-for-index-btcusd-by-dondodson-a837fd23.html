📉 **Bitcoin – Potential Trend Structure Breakdown (H4)**

On the H4 chart, price has been respecting a clear ascending trendline, consistently forming higher lows and maintaining a bullish structure.

🔍 **What’s changing?**

* The latest upward move resulted in a weaker higher high (sign of exhaustion)
* Followed by a strong bearish reaction
* Price is now approaching a key confluence: **trendline support + EMA (dynamic support)**

⚠️ **Key scenario:**
If price breaks and closes below the trendline and EMA, this would signal a **break of structure (BOS)**. In that case, I expect further downside continuation, potentially targeting the ~66k area.

📊 **Alternative scenario:**
If the trendline holds and we see a strong reaction (e.g. fake breakdown / liquidity grab), continuation to the upside is still in play.

🧠 **Conclusion:**
This is a decision zone — either trend continuation or reversal. Watching closely for confirmation before taking any position.
