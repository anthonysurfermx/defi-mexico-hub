BTC/USD 4H — March 19, 2026 Bias: BULLISH · VWAP Dev +1.41%

103

The chart tells a clean story. BTC has been testing the same resistance zone — the golden band at $73,000–$74,500 — three times since early March. Each test has been rejected. That zone is where sellers are consistently showing up.
Below price, two levels are doing the structural work:

The descending AVWAP (purple line) anchored from the January high at $96k has now curved upward and sits at $69,471. Price is above it — that is why the bias reads BULLISH. This line was resistance for the entire February selloff. It is now support. As long as BTC holds above it, the structure is intact.

Below that, the red horizontal at $68,000 is the hard floor. That level absorbed the February lows and the early March retests. Two touches, held both times.

Current price $70,449 sits in the middle of this structure — above AVWAP support, below the supply zone. The post-Fed pullback from $74,500 brought it back into this range. That is not a breakdown. That is the market digesting the Fed's hawkish repricing in the same $69–74k range it has held for three weeks.
The levels that matter:

$74,500 — break and close above this on volume = next leg
$69,471 — AVWAP support, the line the bull case depends on
$68,000 — hard floor, lose this and the structure changes

Until $74,500 breaks convincingly, this is a range. The bias is bullish because VWAP Dev is positive and the AVWAP is below price — but the supply zone is doing its job. Patience over conviction here.
