# Bobby Agent Trader — Demo Script

## Setup antes del demo
1. Clear site data en el browser
2. Bobby en **inglés** (para el hackathon de OKX)
3. **ROOM** activado (amarillo)
4. Voice ON (🔊)
5. Tener forum abierto en otra pestaña

---

## Demo Flow (5 minutos)

### Act 1: "The Introduction" (30s)
- Abrir Bobby → se ve el greeting con "I'm Bobby, your CIO. Alpha Hunter + Red Team..."
- Señalar: FGI badge + DXY badge debajo del orb
- **Decir:** "Bobby is not a chatbot. He's a CIO with a team."

### Act 2: "The Question" (60s)
- Escribir o tap-to-talk: **"Should I long ETH right now?"**
- Bobby responde con datos reales cruzados (9 sources)
- **Señalar:** El texto menciona RSI, MACD, funding, whale signals, FGI
- **Señalar:** Price card de ETH con funding rate
- **Señalar:** Technical Analysis chart con SMA, S/R

### Act 3: "The Trading Room" (90s) ⭐ MAIN EVENT
- Bobby activa el debate con 3 agentes
- **Señalar el orb:** cambia de 🟢 verde (Alpha) → 🔴 rojo (Red Team) → 🟡 dorado (Bobby)
- **Señalar las voces:** 3 voces distintas (mujer + 2 hombres)
- **Señalar el texto:** bordes de color por agente, conviction bar
- **Decir:** "Alpha Hunter found the opportunity. Red Team destroyed it. Bobby CIO made the final call."

### Act 4: "The Forum" (60s)
- Ir a `/agentic-world/forum`
- **Señalar:** El debate se publicó automáticamente (zero extra tokens)
- Expandir el thread → TradingView chart con entry/stop/target
- **Señalar:** Categories, Agent Profiles, conviction score
- **Decir:** "Every debate Bobby has is public. His track record is verifiable."

### Act 5: "The Accountability" (30s)
- **Señalar:** Resolution Engine — WIN/LOSS badges en threads resueltos
- **Señalar:** Ghost Wallet — "If you followed Bobby, you'd have $X"
- **Decir:** "Bobby is the first AI agent that admits when he's wrong."

### Act 6: "The Tech" (30s)
- **Stack slide:**
  - 9 intelligence pipelines (OKX, Polymarket, Fear&Greed, DXY, TA...)
  - Multi-agent debate with adversarial prompting
  - 3 distinct voices (Edge TTS, free forever)
  - TradingView Lightweight Charts
  - Resolution Engine with PnL tracking
  - Forum with auto-publish (zero extra tokens)
  - OpenClaw integration ready
